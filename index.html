Proposal Document Checklists

Answer a few questions about your proposal and get a personalized list of every document you need — with your exact internal deadline dates calculated automatically.

NIH
NIH Proposal Document Checklist
For R01, R21, R03, and other NIH mechanisms. Covers human subjects, clinical trials, genomic data, subrecipients, and more.
Calculates your 5-day & 10-day DRA deadlines
All 4 human subjects documents when applicable
Resource Sharing Plan, DMS Plan, model organisms
PI vs. CSWORA ownership on every document
Open NIH Checklist
NSF
NSF Proposal Document Checklist
For Standard Research, CAREER, RAPID/EAGER, and collaborative proposals. Based on PAPPG 24-1 and Policy Notice Supplement 2 (Jan 2026).
Calculates your 5-day & 10-day DRA deadlines
COA forms, Synergistic Activities (now separate)
Simultaneous collaborative & subaward logic
NSF-specific warnings (no letters of support, etc.)
Open NSF Checklist

How it works

1
Enter your deadline
Type in your sponsor deadline date and choose the 10-day or 5-day track.
2
Answer questions
~15 questions about your proposal type, compliance needs, and personnel.
3
Get your checklist
A personalized list of required documents — with exact due dates on each one.
4
Print or share
Print a clean copy or share this page link with your co-investigators.
What the tools calculate
✓All internal CSWORA / DRA deadline dates from your sponsor deadline
✓Accounts for weekends and all 11 federal holidays
✓Which documents you need vs. which are not applicable
✓Who is responsible for each document — PI vs. CSWORA
✓Due date for every document based on your track selection
✓Sponsor-specific warnings (NSF: no letters of support, etc.)
